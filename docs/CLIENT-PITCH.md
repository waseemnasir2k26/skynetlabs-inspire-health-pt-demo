# Client Pitch Log — Inspire Health PT (Fiverr)

**Demo:** https://skynetlabs-inspire-health-pt-demo.vercel.app
**Brief:** WordPress + WooCommerce/Stripe ebook checkout + Calendly + VSL + email capture. Pages: Home, About, Services, Eval LP, Ebook LP, Thank-You. Fixed up to $800, up to 2 weeks.

## Their explicit questions → our answers

**1. Examples of landing pages & paywalls?**
→ Live in the demo: Evaluation LP (`#/v1/evaluation`) = VSL + booking flow + lead capture. Ebook LP (`#/v1/ebook`) = working Stripe-style paywall (email + card + "Pay $19"). Both built specifically for *this* clinic, not a portfolio link.

**2. WooCommerce experience?**
→ Yes. Ebook ships as a WooCommerce **downloadable/virtual product** with **Stripe** gateway; purchase triggers an automated order-complete email with a secure, expiring download link. (Full mapping in `WORDPRESS-PORT.md`.)

**3. Estimated timeline?**
→ Inside the 2-week window. Wk 1: WordPress build of approved variant (all pages + WooCommerce/Stripe + Calendly + email automation). Wk 2: content load, responsive + speed pass, QA, launch.

**4. Revisions?**
→ Two rounds included. The 3-variant demo collapses most revision cycles up front — you pick the direction before the WP build starts.

**5. Preferred page builder / build method?**
→ **Kadence** child theme (clean markup, fast Core Web Vitals) over Divi/Elementor bloat. The demo's design tokens port 1:1.

## Scope-unlocking questions (sent — 6, one is a pricing lever)

1. Which of the three demo styles is closest to your brand guide — Editorial (warm), Cinematic (dark), or Minimal (clean)? Or a blend?
2. Do you already have the WordPress hosting + domain, or should that be part of setup?
3. For the ebook: one PDF at one price, or are you planning a small product line (bundles/upsells) later? *(pricing/scope lever)*
4. For bookings — is your Calendly already collecting the right intake fields, and is it HIPAA-configured (BAA) if you collect any health info?
5. Which email tool sends the ebook + follow-ups (Mailchimp, FluentCRM, GoHighLevel, other)?
6. Can you share the brand guide, clinic photos, VSL links, testimonials (with the patient's written permission), and the ebook PDF so I can drop real assets straight in?

## Notes / positioning
- Lead with **V1 Editorial (warm-luxe)** — separates instantly from every competitor's clinical teal/navy.
- Compliance baked in (no cure claims, HIPAA-safe forms, superbill framing, disclaimers) — flag the pre-launch FL Board of PT + attorney review.
- Phase 2 upsell: per-service SEO landing pages, Google Business Profile + local SEO, review-capture automation, monthly care plan / membership funnel.
